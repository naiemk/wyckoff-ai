BY REACHING  KEY  LEVELS
It is one more way to evaluate this law of effort and result; thi s time, in terms
of broken levels.
It is simple: if you approach a level with volume and make an
effective break we will say that there is harmony effort/result  in that breakout
movement. That volume was intended to move forward and has absorbed all
the orders that were placed there.
If, on the other hand, you approach a level with volume and make a
false break, we will say that there is divergence. All of that ope rated volume
has been participating in the opposite direction to the level break.
