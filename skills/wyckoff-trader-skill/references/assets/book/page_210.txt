THE CONCEPT OF REVERSAL OF MOVEMENT
Determining the end of a longer movement is not easy. The goal is to identify
as early as possible the point at which the beginning of a movement in the
opposite direction is likely to occur.

movement in which the price currently stands. And let's assume that t his
