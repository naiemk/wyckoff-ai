CONCLUSION
Regardless of the origin of the purchase or sale order ( trader retail ,
institutional, algorithm and so on.) the result is that liquidity is added to the
market; and this is what is really important when trading.
Two of the tools we can use to understand the result of this interaction
between supply and demand are price and volume.
It is necessary to develop the ability to correctly interpret the pr ice
action with respect to its volume if we want to know at all time s what is
happening in the market.
This is why I consider the Wyckoff methodology to be a really solid
approach when analyzing what is happening in the graph (accumulation and
distribution processes) and making judicious scenarios.
