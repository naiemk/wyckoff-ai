IN THE  DEVELOPMENT  OF A CANDLE
It's the simplest evaluation. We try to analyze the price and volume action in
a simple individual candle.
Candles are the final representation of a battle between buyers and
sellers within a certain period of time.
The final result of this interaction between supply and demand sends
us a message.
Our job as traders who analyze the action of price and volume is to
know how to interpret that message correctly. In this case in isolation.
