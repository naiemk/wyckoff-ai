What are the reasons that lead the ill-informed trader to provide t he
liquidity that large traders need? We recall the origin of those li quidity
providers already seen in the preliminary stop event:
The greedy . There will be a group who see the climax
movement and enter the market for fear of losing it.
The fearful . Another group, generally with medium- to
long-term positions, will have stockpiled and will have withstood
much of the previous trend movement. They are in losses and
when they see a new movement against them they decide to close
their positions to avoid greater losses.
The smart ones . A last group of traders, believing
themselves to be the smartest in the class, will want to antic ipate
the turn and at that point they will probably already find
themselves with open positions. This third type of counterparty
takes place when the protection stop of these positions is skipped.
