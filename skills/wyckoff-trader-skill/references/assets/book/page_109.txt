BUYING CLIMAX
Buying Climax is a powerful sign of market weakness. After an uptrend, t he
price, guided by favorable news and a buying irrationality on the part of the
participants (ill-informed) will cause a rapid rise.
At this point, the market will have reached an uninteresting level to
stay within, and well-informed traders will abandon their buying positions
and even begin to position themselves short expecting lower prices.
The Buying Climax is the second event that appears after the
Preliminary Supply and takes place within the Phase A stop of the previ ous
bullish trend.
This climatic movement is originated by professional traders capable
of initiating a price shift; and is followed by ill-informed trader s who make
their operational decisions generally on the basis of their emotions.
