PROJECTION
With this tool we evaluate the distance that the impulses travel and compare
them with the previous ones to determine whether the strength has incre ased
or decreased.
For a trend to remain alive, each impulse must surpass the previous
impulse. If an impulse is not able to make new progress in the direction of the
trend, it is an alert that the movement may be reaching its end.
Distance of the impulse:
The distance between 3 and 4 is greater than the distance
between 1 and 2 = trend strength.
The distance between 5 and 6 is less than the distance
between 3 and 4 = trend weakness.
