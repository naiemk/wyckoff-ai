At this point I would like to emphasize again the importance of
quantifying our entry trigger. As we see, there are many ways to enter the

My recommendation is that you acquire the knowledge to perform a robust
backtesting process in order to obtain the statistics that will provi de us with
objective results. This way we can compare the performance of the di fferent
ways we analyze to enter.
