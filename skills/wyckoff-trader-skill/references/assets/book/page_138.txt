PATTERN OF 2 OR MORE CANDLES
The background of the action is exactly the same as for the example of a
candle. The only difference is that on this possibility the behavior develops
over a greater temporal space.
The fact that the price takes longer before reversing and recovering
the previously established breakout zone is a sign of less strength for t he
shock. In other words, the less time the turn takes place, the greater  the
strength will denote the shock.
