reaction that will visually leave a large movement in the opposite di rection,
thus confirming the climatic event.
This movement will be the most important since the market began the
previous trend Phase. It suggests an aggressive entry of traders in the

This ChoCh (Change of Character) has great implications and is that
it signals a change in the context of the market; the ChoCh appears to put an
end to the previous trend and start an environment of lateralization in t he
price.
This change in behavior must be confirmed with the last event of
