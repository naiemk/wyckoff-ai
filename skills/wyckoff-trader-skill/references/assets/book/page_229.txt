 
S&P500 INDEX ($ES)
On the weekly chart we see a classic reaccumulation structure with a shock.
This is a very good example to see the visual representation of what a
Buying Exhaustion is. We see how the upward movement comes to an end
without a volume peak that would identify the climatic event. With the
Automatic Reaction and the Secondary Test the Phase A stop would be
completed.
During Phase B we already see certain background strength in
developing this test at highs (Upthrust Action). Action that originates t he test
event in Phase C (Shakeout) with a relatively high volume. In this first part of
Phase B we can see how the volume in general decreases, a sign that  there is
an absorption of stock by the buyers.
