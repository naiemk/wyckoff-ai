DEPTH
With depth analysis we evaluate the distance travelled by trend rever sals to
determine whether weakness has increased or decreased.
As with projection analysis, we can evaluate depth using two
measurements: the total distance of the recoil from its origin t o its end; and
the distance the price travels from the previous end to the new end.
