After the Major Sign of Weakness, a brief upward reversal (Last Point
of Supply) serves as a distribution confirmation test and gives rise t o Phase E
where the price quickly develops the downward movement out of range.
During the creation of the structure the overall volume remains
relatively high, trace of the distribution ranges. In addition, Weis waves show
the loss of momentum in the upward movement and an increase in the
downward movement, being very noticeable in the last stages of the structure.
