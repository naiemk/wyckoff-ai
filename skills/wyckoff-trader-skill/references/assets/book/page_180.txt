CHAPTER 21 - PHASE A: STOPPING THE  
PREVIOUS TREND
 
The main function of this Phase is to stop the previous trend movement and
return the market to a state of balance between the forces of suppl y and
demand, or between buyers and sellers. We move from a trend context to a
range context.
Phase A consists of the first four events:
Preliminary Support and Preliminary Supply
Selling Climax and Buying Climax
Automatic Rally and Automatic Reaction
Secondary Test

side. As we know, a control of the sellers will be represented as a downtrend;
